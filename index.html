About Me

Hi! My name is John. A few years ago I retired from the healthcare industry after 25 rewarding years as a Magnetic Resonance Imaging Technologist. I fiddled around with different smaller jobs mostly working outdoors, but soon felt I was missing something. Knowing I still have a lot to contribute, the ambitious side of me started snooping around computer programming as this has always piqued my intrest. With a little research, I decided to enroll in a Full-Stack Web Development program offered at Denver University. I hope to find a career in web development after completion of the course in six months. Ultimately, I would like something remote but understand my options may be limited as a junior web developer. This career is how I intend to spend the remainder of my working life and I look forward to an exciting future with many options!
